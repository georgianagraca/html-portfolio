About Me

I am a computer science student and an art lover. This portfolio is a compilation of my growth as both a professional and an artist passionate and the gallery below showcase samples of projects further developed in the pages of this portfolio.

I am Georgiana Maria, and I am a second-year Computer Science student at the Federal University of Paraíba. I am from João Pessoa, Brazil, and this website is a little window into what I have learned, done, and created during my first few years in college while pursuing my degree. On the professional side, I have included the main projects I have been a part of through classes, internships, and clubs on campus.

I have also been involved with art for many years, exploring different dance styles and acting techniques. These are my main hobbies since I was a kid, and I have included some of my favorite works. I believe that creating and maintaining an analytical perspective on my art while learning from it makes me a better professional, as it ties into concepts of creativity in general, and vice versa.
